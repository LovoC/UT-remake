Undertale Guide

A comprehensive guide to the world of Undertale

Overview

Undertale is an indie RPG created by Toby Fox, released in 2015. The game follows a human who falls into the Underground, a hidden world inhabited by monsters who were sealed away from the surface long ago.

⚔️

Your Choices Matter

Every decision shapes your journey and relationships with characters.

Unlike traditional RPGs, Undertale allows complete freedom in how you interact with the world. You can choose to fight, show mercy, or find creative solutions to conflicts. These choices have lasting consequences on the story and characters.
❤️

Unique Combat

Turn-based RPG meets bullet-hell gameplay in creative battles.

The combat system combines classic turn-based mechanics with bullet-hell sequences. Control your SOUL to dodge enemy attacks while choosing to FIGHT, ACT, use ITEMS, or show MERCY. Each encounter reveals something about the characters.
📖

Memorable Story

Experience humor, emotion, and deep themes throughout your journey.

Undertale weaves humor with heartfelt moments and explores themes of friendship, loneliness, determination, and consequences. The game challenges traditional RPG concepts and encourages players to think about their impact on the world.
👥

Unforgettable Characters

Meet quirky, lovable monsters with unique personalities and stories.

From the skeleton brothers Sans and Papyrus to the determined Undyne and shy Alphys, each character has depth, personality, and their own struggles. Your interactions with them can lead to friendship, rivalry, or something in between.

Characters

The Underground is home to many unique monsters, each with their own personalities, goals, and stories. Every character has their own history and perspective on the world they live in.

Throughout the journey, the player meets characters who can become friends, enemies, or something in between depending on their choices. The way the player interacts with them can completely change their relationships and the events that follow.

From the cheerful and determined Papyrus, to the mysterious and laid-back Sans, every character plays an important role in shaping the world of Undertale. Some characters guide the player, while others challenge their beliefs and decisions.

The game focuses heavily on the connections made between humans and monsters. Conversations, actions, and even small decisions can reveal different sides of each character, making the Underground feel like a living world rather than just a place to explore.

Many characters also have their own personal struggles, dreams, and fears. Understanding their stories is an important part of discovering the truth behind the Underground and the events that led to its creation.

This section will explore the main characters of Undertale, including their personalities, roles in the story, and how they are affected by the player's choices.

Meet the Characters

Frisk

A human child who fell into the Underground. Their actions and choices shape the fate of both humans and monsters, as they navigate a world where violence isn't the only option. Silent yet expressive, their red SOUL represents the determination they have in order to continue.

Chara

The first human to fall into the Underground, adopted by the Dreemurr family. Their mysterious past and tragic fate are intertwined with the Underground's history. Their red SOUL symbolizes determination, but their true nature remains one of the game's deepest mysteries.

Flowey

A sentient golden flower with a twisted smile who greets you at the start of your journey. Seemingly friendly at first, he hides dark intentions and shocking secrets. His famous phrase "In this world, it's kill or be killed" sets a chilling tone for what lies ahead.

Toriel

The motherly caretaker of the Ruins who protects fallen humans from the dangers ahead. Once the queen of the Underground, she now lives in isolation, baking pies and solving puzzles. Her kindness masks a deep sadness about the past she cannot change.

Sans

A lazy skeleton who loves puns, pranks, and hanging out at Grillby's. Despite his carefree attitude, he possesses mysterious knowledge and incredible power. His laid-back demeanor hides a deeper understanding of the world's nature and a tragic awareness of his reality.

Papyrus

An enthusiastic skeleton who dreams of joining the Royal Guard and making friends. His confidence, cooking skills (especially spaghetti), and elaborate puzzles showcase his determination to prove himself. Despite his bravado, he's incredibly kind-hearted and believes in everyone's potential for good.

Undyne

The fierce captain of the Royal Guard with unwavering determination and loyalty to monsterkind. Her intense personality and incredible strength make her a formidable warrior, but beneath her tough exterior lies a passionate heart. She mentors Papyrus and shares a close friendship with Alphys.

Alphys

The royal scientist who's obsessed with anime, particularly Mew Mew Kissy Cutie. Shy and socially awkward, she struggles with anxiety and guilt over her past experiments. Despite her insecurities, her scientific genius and genuine care for others shine through, especially in her relationship with Undyne.

Mettaton

A glamorous robot entertainer and the Underground's biggest celebrity. Originally built by Alphys, he's a charismatic performer who loves the spotlight and his adoring fans. His shows range from cooking segments to dramatic quiz shows, all designed to captivate his audience—and capture human SOULs.

Asgore

The gentle king of monsters who tends to his golden flowers in the throne room. Burdened by the weight of his crown and the deaths of his children, he made a terrible vow to free monsterkind by collecting human SOULs. Despite his imposing presence, he's kind, loves tea, and hopes for a peaceful resolution.

Asriel

The lost prince of the Underground and son of Asgore and Toriel. His tragic story and the events surrounding his death shaped the entire conflict between humans and monsters. Compassionate and innocent, his fate connects to the deepest secrets of the Underground and the true nature of determination.

Gameplay

Undertale combines traditional RPG elements with unique mechanics that change how players approach battles. Your decisions shape the journey through different routes:

Neutral Route

+

The Neutral Route is the result of making mixed choices throughout the game. It happens when you fight some enemies but spare others, leading to many possible endings based on your specific actions.

  • Multiple Endings: Over a dozen different outcomes
  • Natural Playthrough: Most first-time players experience this
  • Varied Relationships: Mixed friendships and conflicts
  • Phone Calls: Unique ending calls based on who you spared

Pacifist Route

+

The Pacifist Route focuses on showing mercy to every monster and forming connections with the characters. It reveals the most complete story and explores the deepest relationships between humans and monsters.

  • True Ending: The canonical "best" ending
  • Zero Kills: Spare every enemy without exception
  • Befriend Everyone: Complete friendship sidequests
  • True Lab: Unlock hidden areas and secrets

Genocide Route

+

The Genocide Route happens when you choose a much darker path, systematically defeating every enemy in each area. This route dramatically changes the world, characters, and overall tone of the game.

  • Complete Extinction: Exhaust every encounter
  • Hardest Battles: Face incredibly difficult boss fights
  • Permanent Consequences: Actions affect future playthroughs
  • Dark Narrative: Experience the game's darkest story

Story

The story of Undertale begins when a human falls into the Underground, a hidden world beneath the surface where monsters have been trapped for many years.

1

The War

Long ago, humans and monsters lived together peacefully...

Long ago, humans and monsters lived together peacefully. However, after a conflict between the two races, humans emerged victorious and sealed the monsters away using a powerful barrier created by seven human mages, separating them from the outside world forever.

2

The First Human

201X: A human child fell into the Underground...

The first human to fall into the Underground after the war was adopted by the royal family, Asgore and Toriel. This child, later known as Chara, formed a close bond with the king and queen's son, Asriel. Together, they dreamed of freeing monsterkind and seeing the flowers on the surface.

3

The Tragedy

A plan gone wrong led to the deaths of both children...

Chara became gravely ill and died. Asriel absorbed Chara's SOUL and crossed the barrier to the surface. However, when humans attacked Asriel, he refused to fight back and returned to the Underground, where he died and turned to dust. This tragedy shattered the royal family and changed the Underground forever.

4

The Decree

Asgore declared war on humanity...

In his grief and rage, King Asgore declared that any human who falls into the Underground would be killed, and their SOUL collected. Seven human SOULs would be needed to break the barrier. Queen Toriel, unable to accept this decree, left Asgore and isolated herself in the Ruins to protect any humans who might fall.

5

Your Journey

You fall into the Underground and your story begins...

You arrive in the Underground as the eighth human to fall since the war. The choices you make will determine not only your fate but the fate of all monsters trapped beneath the surface. Will you show mercy and break the cycle of violence, or will you follow a darker path? Every decision matters, and the Underground remembers.
